How To Play

When the game starts, you will be presented
with a 3-5 letter word. You will have to
memorize which colors correspond to each letter.

After a short period of time, you will have to
match each color with its corresponding letter.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move from
block to block. After inputting all letters, press
the "Enter" key to submit your guess.

Match all the correct letters, and move on to the next word.
However, mismatch just one letter, and the game is over!
